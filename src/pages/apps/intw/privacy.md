"I Need That Widget" displays your calendar events through iOS widgets.

**What the app does:**
- Reads your calendar events to display in widgets
- All data remains on your device

**What the app doesn't do:**
- No data is collected, stored, or shared
- No calendar events are modified or deleted
- No personal information is transmitted anywhere
